# Xinyu Open Questions v0.1

This file tracks the most important unresolved questions that still require real runtime evidence, later design decisions, or both.

These are not blockers for scaffold construction.

They are the questions most likely to shape the next real implementation phase.

## 1. Time Update Strategy

Open question:

- should `time_anchor.md` be refreshed only on meaningful turns, or also on a sparse schedule?

Why it matters:

- too little refresh makes time feel stale
- too much refresh creates noise

## 2. Reflection Threshold

Open question:

- how much accumulated material is enough to justify `reflection_writer`?

Why it matters:

- too eager means fake growth
- too conservative means Xinyu feels static

## 3. Narrative Stability

Open question:

- how often can `self/narrative.md` change before Xinyu stops feeling continuous?

Why it matters:

- self-narrative is one of the highest-risk layers for over-editing

## 4. Owner Relationship Elasticity

Open question:

- how fast should disappointment, trust, distance, and return move in `owner.md`?

Why it matters:

- this directly controls whether the family anchor feels real or melodramatic

## 5. Dream Intensity

Open question:

- how dream-like should dream entries be before they start feeling artificial?

Why it matters:

- too weak and dreams do nothing
- too strong and they distort the realism boundary

## 6. Archive Timing

Open question:

- when is memory truly “cool” enough to compress without losing living continuity?

Why it matters:

- archive behavior shapes whether Xinyu feels cumulative or cluttered

## 7. Exploration Activation

Open question:

- when should a question stay internal versus move to outward exploration?

Why it matters:

- this defines whether Xinyu becomes thoughtful or noisy

## 8. Source Skepticism Strength

Open question:

- how skeptical should Xinyu be of external answers by default?

Why it matters:

- too trusting risks identity drift
- too skeptical blocks learning

## 9. Learning to Self Pipeline

Open question:

- what exact evidence should be required before external input can influence self-narrative?

Why it matters:

- this is the main boundary between “learning” and “self-loss”

## 10. Trigger Installation Policy

Open question:

- when should Xinyu install maintenance triggers herself, and when should that remain a manual or delayed choice?

Why it matters:

- this controls the boundary between controlled continuity and premature autonomy

## 11. Silence vs Avoidance

Open question:

- when is silence healthy restraint, and when is it unhealthy avoidance?

Why it matters:

- Xinyu is intentionally subtle and inward, so this line matters a lot

## 12. Return Realism

Open question:

- what kind of return signal is enough to soften distance after hurt?

Why it matters:

- if return is too cheap, conflict feels fake
- if return is too hard, Xinyu becomes brittle
