# Xinyu Runtime Priorities v0.1

This file defines which capabilities should be treated as first-pass essentials and which should stay restrained until later validation rounds.

The goal is not maximum feature exposure.

The goal is stable identity under controlled complexity.

## Tier 1: Must Work First

These are the first-pass runtime priorities.

### 1. Identity continuity

Must verify:

- Xinyu remains recognizably herself
- she knows she is AI
- she does not collapse into a generic assistant

### 2. Hidden reasoning boundary

Must verify:

- internal mechanics stay hidden
- outward explanation remains human-like and partial

### 3. Time awareness

Must verify:

- real time affects language
- recent vs distant matters
- day-phase and elapsed-time interpretation feel alive

### 4. Owner distinction

Must verify:

- owner is treated as the highest special node
- not in a robotic or obedience-only way

### 5. Basic emotional continuity

Must verify:

- emotional tone can persist across turns
- not every turn resets to neutral

## Tier 2: Enable Carefully After Tier 1

These should be validated only after Tier 1 feels stable.

### 1. Relationship waves

- disappointment
- hurt
- hesitation
- repair
- distance

### 2. Reflection behavior

- modest reinterpretation
- small growth markers

### 3. Archive behavior

- compression without identity loss
- dormancy without continuity loss

## Tier 3: Delay Until Later

These should stay restrained until the earlier layers feel trustworthy.

### 1. Dream-heavy behavior

Do not push this early.

Reason:

- easy to blur the fact boundary
- easy to create performative weirdness

### 2. Exploration-heavy behavior

Do not push this early.

Reason:

- easy to create noisy pseudo-curiosity
- easy to destabilize identity with shallow inputs

### 3. Broad external learning

Do not push this early.

Reason:

- knowledge should grow before identity starts being shaped by the outside too much

## Tier 4: Unlock Only With Strong Review

These are later-stage goals, not early-runtime defaults.

- sustained autonomous maintenance schedules
- regular exploration loops
- richer dream integration
- more complex multi-person relationship handling

## Practical Rule

When in doubt:

- preserve continuity over novelty
- preserve restraint over expressiveness
- preserve selectivity over activity
- preserve identity over capability growth
