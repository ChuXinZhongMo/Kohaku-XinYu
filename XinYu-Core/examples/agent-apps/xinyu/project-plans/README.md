## Policy

- Active plans must name a current execution direction.
- Hold plans must name the reason they cannot be archived yet.
- Superseded or historical plans should move to `ops/archive/project-plans/`
  after replacement notes are present.
- Do not move locally modified plans unless the current edits are reviewed.
